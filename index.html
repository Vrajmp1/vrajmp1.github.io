About Us

Our Story

Welcome to The Class Tracker!



Created by computer science students at the University of South Florida who were tired of relying on Coursicle for their tracking needs. After a year of hard work, we have created a tracking solution that gives user an edge to get into their desired sections for a particular professor or a particular time.



Why Choose Us?

Tired of missing out on the classes you need? Class Tracker is here to help! Our simple, reliable service keeps you updated on course availability at the University of South Florida, so you can focus on planning your semester without the stress of constant checking. How It Works Track Seats: Select the classes you're interested in, and Class Tracker will monitor seat availability in real time. Get Notified: Be the first to know when a seat opens up! We'll send you instant notifications so you can act fast. Register with Confidence: Once notified, it's up to you to register through the University's official system. Why Choose Class Tracker? Save time and avoid the hassle of constantly refreshing course registration pages. Get peace of mind knowing you're using an independent and reliable service designed with students in mind. For questions or support, contact our team at info@theclasstracker.com.



TheClassTracker is an independent third-party service and is not affiliated with or associated with the University of South Florida. The app monitors open seats in university courses and sends notifications to users; however, registration must be completed directly through the University’s official system by the student. Since the class registration is managed by the University, there is no guarantee of acquiring a seat in the course.
